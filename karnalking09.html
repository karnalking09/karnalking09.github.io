Local lab: Docker Compose (OWASP Juice Shop)

Copy into docker-compose.yml and run docker-compose up -d.

version: "3"
services:
juice-shop:
image: bkimminich/juice-shop:latest
ports:
- "3000:3000"
restart: unless-stopped

Access the lab at http://localhost:3000. Practice there — do not test real third-party systems without permission.
